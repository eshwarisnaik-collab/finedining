Our story

Built around one hearth,
not a hundred burners.

01

The Hearth — est. 2018

  • Oak & fruitwoodFuel
  • Open kitchenFormat
  • Farm-directSourcing

Ember & Oak started as a single wood-fired grill and a short, stubborn menu. We believed a kitchen didn't need a wall of gas burners to be serious — it needed patience, good wood, and produce worth cooking simply.

Today the hearth still sits at the centre of the room. Every course, from the first snack to the last dessert, passes near the fire in some form — smoked, charred, roasted, or finished over embers. Our team works in close rotation between the pass and the dining room, so the people plating your food are often the ones bringing it to your table.

We keep the menu short and seasonal on purpose. Shorter menus mean better ingredients, tighter technique, and a kitchen that can actually taste everything it sends out.
